THE QUESTION AND COLLECTION OF DATA

We sent a google form to people primarily but not exclucivly based in the us through social media. We did this so that we could see each grade level's plans after highschool to see if their plans varied by grade level.

THE DATA'S EXPLAINATION

Last slide

The data was colected with google sheets, filtered by grade level, and then by one of the six plans we picked after highschool. We decided to use a grouped bar chart to visualize our data. The "groups" are the post-high school plans, and the bars within each group represent the number of people in each grade level who have that plan.

Next slide

THE STORY

The data shows that attending a 4 year college is the most common post- graduation plan for each grade level. Not one student in any grade level is planning to go into the military, making it the least popular option.
